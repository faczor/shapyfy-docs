# Problem: Plan Paralysis / Setup Friction

## The Pain Point
**"I'm too lazy to set up the app"**
You download a new workout app. It asks you to "Create Your Routine."
You stare at the blank screen. You have to search for "Bench Press," add it, set the reps, set the rest timer... then do it again for 8 exercises.
"I'll do it later," you say. You never do it later. You delete the app.

## The Shapyfy Solution
**AI Pattern Recognition (The "Magic" Feature)**
Shapyfy is the only app that says: **"Don't set up anything. Just lift."**
1. You log your workouts as you go (using our super-fast logger).
2. After ~2 weeks, our AI notices: "Hey, you do Bench/Flyes/Triceps every Monday."
3. It asks: "Want me to save this as your 'Push Day' plan?"
4. Boom. Your plan is set up. You didn't do any data entry.
