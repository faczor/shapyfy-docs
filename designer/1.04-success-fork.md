# 1.04 - Success + Fork Screen

**Navigation:** [1.03 Exercise Detail](1.03-demo-exercise-detail.md) ‚Üí Success + Fork ‚Üí [1.05 Personalization](1.05-personalization.md) OR [1.09 Exercise Picker](1.09-exercise-picker.md)

## Overview

**Context**: User just logged their first set in the Exercise Detail screen. Auto-navigated here from Exercise Detail. This celebration screen shows what they logged, signals demo completion, and presents the fork decision with clear visual hierarchy.

**Design Philosophy**: Strong visual hierarchy - prominent primary path (gradient accent card), subtle secondary escape hatch (text link). Clean, modern, no emoji clutter.

**Design Revision**: 2025-11-17 v2.1 - Integrated RECOMMENDED badge into gradient bar header (left 40%) for instant recognition and zero text competition. Badge and gradient form unified visual signal.

**Previous Revisions**:
- 2025-11-17 v2 - Fixed accessibility violations (contrast ratios, touch targets, reduced-motion), reverted gradient to light‚Üídark for spatial metaphor consistency, reduced animation from 750ms to 400ms, strengthened fork hierarchy with RECOMMENDED badge and background tint, standardized typography (14/16/18/28sp) and spacing (16/24/32/48dp) systems.
- 2025-01-06 v1 - Redesigned from two equal cards to single prominent primary card + text link secondary. Added demo closure messaging ("You've learned the basics") to clarify transition from demo to real app usage.

**Interaction & Motion**
The success moment is emphasized with a confetti-like burst and a bold checkmark animation. The primary "Help Me Build" option pulses slightly to draw attention, while the secondary option remains subtle.
