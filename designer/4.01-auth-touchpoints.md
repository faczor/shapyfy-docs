## 2. Human-Readable Summary
**User Behavior**
We don't force users to sign up immediately. Instead, we invite them at key moments of value ("Touchpoints").
*   **Touchpoint 1 (Early)**: A subtle banner after the first demo workout.
*   **Touchpoint 2 (First Milestone)**: A bottom sheet after the first *real* workout, emphasizing backup.
*   **Touchpoint 3 (Streak)**: A full-screen celebration after 3 workouts, emphasizing "being on fire" and unlocking features.
*   **Touchpoint 4 (Passive)**: A persistent option in the Settings menu.

**Key Cases**
*   **Dismissal**: Users can always dismiss these prompts (except the settings menu item).
*   **Progression**: Once a user creates an account, these touchpoints disappear or change state.

**Data Displayed**
*   **Personalized Stats**: "You just logged [X] kg!" or "3 Workouts completed".
*   **Value Props**: Backup, Sync, Early Access.

**Interaction & Motion**
Transitions between authentication states are seamless. The login form slides in smoothly, and error messages appear with a gentle shake, guiding the user without frustration. Successful login transitions to the main app with a welcoming fade-in.
