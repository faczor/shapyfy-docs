## 2. Human-Readable Summary
**User Behavior**
The user answers 4 quick questions to help the app build a personalized workout plan. They select options for their goal, workout location, experience level, and training frequency. As they answer, a progress bar fills up. Once all questions are answered, they can continue to see their recommended plan.

**Key Cases**
*   **Answering Questions**: Tapping an option selects it and automatically scrolls to the next question if needed.
*   **Completion**: The "Continue" button becomes enabled only when all 4 questions are answered.

**Data Displayed**
*   **Question 1**: Main Goal (Build Muscle, Get Stronger, Stay Active, Lose Weight).
*   **Question 2**: Location (Gym, Home, Bodyweight Only).
*   **Question 3**: Experience Level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced).
*   **Question 4**: Training Frequency (2-6 days/week).
*   **Progress**: 4-dot indicator showing completion status.

## 4. Notes
*   **Design Philosophy**: Clean, focused, and efficient. Prioritizes user intent (Goal) over logistics.
*   **Accessibility Features**:
    *   **WCAG AA/AAA Compliance**: High contrast text (up to 16.1:1).
    *   **Touch Targets**: 48dp+ minimum.
    *   **Screen Reader**: Dynamic progress updates ("1 of 4 questions complete").
*   **Developer Notes**:
    *   **State Management**: Track `goal`, `equipment`, `experience`, `frequency`.
    *   **Smart Scroll**: Auto-scroll to next question only if needed.
    *   **Responsiveness**: Hide progress dots on very short screens (< 444dp).
    *   **API**: POST to `/api/recommendations/workout-plans` on continue.
